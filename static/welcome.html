The Laboratorio Nacional de Análisis y Síntesis Ecológica is a Multidisciplinary Scientific Research Center created in 2015 as part of the program of Laboratorios Nacionales CONACyT, whose purpose is the analysis and synthesis of ecological information on genetic resources that use state of the art technology such as genomics and bioinformatics.


Galaxy is an open platform for supporting data intensive research. Galaxy is developed by The Galaxy Team with the support of many contributors.

The Galaxy Project is supported in part by NHGRI, NSF, The Huck Institutes of the Life Sciences, The Institute for CyberScience at Penn State, and Johns Hopkins University.
